About Me

I am Mahtab Nejati, a M.Sc. student in Software Engineering at Sharif University of Technology (SUT) and a researcher in Automated Software Engineering (ASE) lab under the supervision of Dr. Abbas Heydarnoori. I graduated a B.Sc. program in Electrical Engineering, majoring in Electronics, from Khaje Nasir Toosi University of Technology (KNTU).
I have previously worked on a user review analysis project for release planing. Now, I'm working on a solution for categorization of GitHub (or any other software repository) projects alongside Maliheh Izadi.
